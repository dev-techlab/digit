1. Manual Overview

This manual helps DLink distributors use the backend system for daily operations.

It explains backend menus, player-side mappings, and common operating scenarios so users can understand each function, operation steps, impact, and key notes.

2.2 Player Registration and Attribution

Players register through a distributor or agent invite link, poster QR code, or Kiosk entry.

After registration, players are attributed to the corresponding Store, Sale Agent, and Sub Agent. Their future deposits, game transfers, redemptions, bonuses, and tickets are recorded under the related backend account.

Key Rule

Do not mix QR codes, Invite Links, or posters from different stores or agents. Incorrect attribution may affect future earnings and support handling.

2.3 Balance and Currency Concepts

Field / Currency

Meaning

Usage Notes

Gold Coins / GC

Entertainment coins only; not redeemable.

The player side clearly states: Gold Coins cannot be redeemed.

Sweepstakes Coins / SC

SC used for promotions or redemption.

Usually divided into Unplayed and Redeemable.

Redeemable SC

SC that becomes redeemable after conditions are met.

Do not call it cash when speaking to players.

Bonus SC

Reward SC from promotions, rewards, phone binding, and similar actions.

Affects promotion cost and player rewards.

2.4 Common Operation Terms

Term

Note

Deposit

In the player wallet, it means adding funds.

In games, it usually means transferring online balance into a game.

Redeem

Not a direct withdrawal to a bank card; it means moving funds from a game account back to online redeemable balance.

Withdraw

A player or agent withdraws available balance to an external account.

Transfer

Balance transfer between agents or player-related accounts.

Online to Game

Online balance transferred into a game.

Game to Online

Game balance transferred back online.

5.3 Bind or Change Email

Bind Email:

1. On first login, the system prompts you to bind an Email.

2. Enter a valid Email, click Send Code, and enter the 6-digit code to bind it.

Change Email:

1. Click Change Email on Dashboard.

2. The Security Verification window appears.

3. Click Send Code to send a code to the current Email.

4. Enter the 6-digit code.

5. Click Verify, then complete the Email change.

Notes

Email is required for financial operations, account security, and system notices.

If the current Email cannot receive codes, contact platform support. Do not switch directly to an employee’s personal Email.

5.4 Invite Link

Invite Link is used for distributor or store promotion. Players who register through this link are attributed to the current account.

Click the copy icon beside the Invite Link, then share it by SMS, WhatsApp, Facebook, or other channels.

You can also download QR-code posters, print and display them, or share them online.

7. Game Setting

Figure 6: Game Setting

7.1 Function Overview

Game Setting configures the game platform accounts available to the current store or agent. It directly controls which games players can use.

The page has two sections:

1. Automatic score adjustment games: the system can add or deduct scores automatically after the game store account is configured.

2. Manual score adjustment games: manual handling is required, or D-Link support must enable automatic mode.

Permission Note

The page shows “Game settings are managed by your agent. Contact your agent for changes.” Some game settings are managed by an upstream agent or the platform, so the current account may not have full edit access.

12. Promotion Config

Promotion Config controls player-side Bonus display, reward triggers, reward caps, applicable games, and user scope.

Promotion settings directly affect player rewards, platform costs, and distributor earnings.

12.1 Promotion Types and Player-Side Display

Backend Promotion Type

Function

Promotion Game (100% Bonus)

Player receives a 100% bonus on the first deposit of the day, capped at $50.00.

Double Game Bonus

After the first deposit of the day, specified games can receive an extra 100% bonus.

Royalty Drop

After meeting the weekly deposit threshold, the player receives a random reward, usually credited to Bonus SC or wallet.

Invite Friends & Earn

After an invited friend meets the first-purchase requirement, both inviter and invitee receive rewards. Some rules may be platform-configured.

Daily Check-in Rewards

Daily check-in reward. It may be a platform-level promotion depending on backend permissions.

Figure 16: Player-Side Invite & Earn Page

18. Common Operations

How to Promote

1. Confirm Store Name and Invite Link.

2. Download posters from Download Posters.

3. Scan the QR code to test the registration page.

4. Display the poster in-store or share it through social channels.

5. Check Member List to confirm new player registration.

How to Confirm a Player Is Attributed to Me

1. Go to Member List.

2. Search by player Username, Phone, or registration time.

3. Check fields such as Sale Agent, Kiosk, and Register Time.

4. Go to Transaction List to view the player’s transactions.

5. After saving settings, check the player-side Bonus Center display.

18.3 What to Do If a Player Deposit Does Not Arrive

1. Check the player’s issue in Help Tickets.

2. Record DLink Acct #, player name, phone, Email, and issue summary.

3. Search in Transaction List by player, amount, and time.

4. Open Transaction Detail to verify status and balance changes.

5. Record the handling process in ticket Notes.

6. Click Mark Resolved after completion.

18.4 What to Do If a Kiosk Is Offline

1. Go to Kiosk List.

2. Check whether Indicator Lights show Offline or Error.

3. Click Detail to check Online Status, Error Status, and Version Status.

4. If needed, go to More > Error or Report to review the issue.

5. Contact device support or submit a ticket.
